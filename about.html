👋 Hi, I'm Mazen Abdul-Knerr

Hi Guys! My name is Mazen Abdul-Knerr, and I'm a rising junior at Northampton High School in Northampton, Massachusetts, USA.

For middle school, I attended The Hartsbrook Waldorf School in Hadley, MA, where I gained unique experiences in both agriculture and the practical arts — including woodworking, blacksmithing, and even knitting.

These hands-on experiences taught me how to approach design and problem-solving from both an artistic and engineering perspective. In PS70 this summer, I hope to continue growing those skills and refining them into the foundation of an engineer’s toolbox.
